# 📊 Product Placement Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

An interactive **Tableau dashboard** analyzing how product placement, pricing, promotions, seasonality, and consumer demographics influence sales performance.
The project helps identify patterns that can assist retailers in optimizing product positioning strategies.

# 🚀 Project Overview

Retail businesses rely heavily on **data-driven insights** to optimize product visibility and maximize sales.
This project analyzes a **Product Positioning dataset** to understand how different factors impact **average sales volume**.

The Tableau dashboard provides an interactive view of:

* Product category performance
* Competitor pricing impact
* Store placement effectiveness
* Seasonal sales trends
* Consumer demographic influence
* Foot traffic patterns

# 📂 Dataset Features

The dataset contains several attributes related to product sales and placement:

* Product Category
* Price
* Competitor's Price
* Product Position (Aisle, End-cap, Front of Store)
* Consumer Demographics
* Promotion
* Seasonal Sales
* Foot Traffic
* Sales Volume

---

# 📈 Visualizations Included

The dashboard contains multiple visual analytics views:

1. **Avg Sales Volume vs Product Category**
   Compares average sales across Clothing, Electronics, and Food.

2. **Competitor Price vs Price**
   Shows how competitor pricing compares with the product price.

3. **Avg Sales Volume by Product Category by Product Position**
   Analyzes the effect of store placement on sales.

4. **Consumer Demographics vs Sales Volume**
   Highlights which demographic groups drive the most sales.

5. **Product Category vs Price**
   Displays pricing trends across different categories.

6. **Avg Sales Volume by Product Category by Season**
   Shows how seasonality affects product performance.

7. **Foot Traffic by Avg Sales Volume**
   Examines how store traffic levels impact sales.

8. **Promotion Impact on Product Category**
   Evaluates how promotions influence sales volume.

---

# 🎛 Dashboard Features

The dashboard is **fully interactive** with filters including:

* Product Category
* Consumer Demographics
* Product Position
* Seasonal Sales
* Foot Traffic

Users can dynamically explore the dataset to uncover insights.

---

# 📊 Key Insights

Some insights obtained from the analysis include:

* **Clothing category generates the highest average sales volume.**
* **Front-of-store placement increases product visibility and sales.**
* **Seasonal changes influence electronics and food sales significantly.**
* **Competitor pricing has a noticeable effect on pricing strategies.**
* **Higher foot traffic correlates with increased sales performance.**
